EU Voter Turnout (2014–2024)

Tools: R (tidyverse, sf), Eurostat Data

A longitudinal analysis of democratic participation across the European Union. This map highlights regional disparities in voter engagement, providing a visual foundation for studying political behavior in member states.

Internal Displacement Dynamics

Tools: R, Spatial Econometrics, ggplot2

Using bivariate mapping techniques, this project visualizes the relationship between conflict intensity and internally displaced populations. It aims to identify high-pressure zones where humanitarian resources are most critical.

Global CO2 Emissions: Per-Capita vs. Total

Tools: R (tidyr), World Bank Data

A comparative spatial study distinguishing between high aggregate emitters and high per-capita emitters. This visualization clarifies the "responsibility gap" in global climate policy by highlighting outliers in carbon efficiency.
